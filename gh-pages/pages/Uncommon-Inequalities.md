$$ |x/y| \ge 1, |y/x| \le 1 or |x/y| \le 1, |y/x| \ge 1 $$

$$ |x/y| + |y/x| \ge 2 $$

proof:

$$ a = max(|x/y|, |y/x|) $$
$$ a >= 1$$
$$ (a - 1)^2 >= 0 $$
$$ a^2 + 1 \ge 2 * a $$
